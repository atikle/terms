Our Commitment to Your Privacy

1. Data Storage: Local-First

My Pocket is built from the ground up to be private. Everything you save in the application is stored exclusively in your web browser's `localStorage` on your own computer or device. We do not have access to this data *unless you choose to use a feature that transmits it.*

2. Optional Feature: Email Your Items

We provide an optional feature to "Email My Items." Using this feature is your choice and constitutes your **explicit consent** for us to process and transmit your data as described here.

  • What is Shared: When you use this feature, you are requesting that we send a copy of all your saved items to the email address you provide.
  • Third-Party Services: To provide this service, your data (saved items and the email address you provide) is sent to and processed by our backend service (a Google Apps Script) and transmitted via Google's email servers (Gmail). These services have their own privacy policies.
  • Security: We cannot guarantee the security of email as a transmission medium. Email is not end-to-end encrypted and may be intercepted by third parties. You use this feature at your own risk.
  • Data Handling: Your email address and data are processed by these services solely for the purpose of sending you the requested email. We may temporarily log request details (like the date and recipient email) for troubleshooting and abuse prevention, which are stored in Google Sheet logs.

3. Data Safety and Backups

Since your primary data lives only in your browser's storage, it is tied to that specific browser and device. This means actions like clearing your browser's cache or site data will permanently delete your My Pocket items. We don't have a copy (other than what you optionally send via email), so we cannot help you recover them. Please be mindful of this and consider using the 'Export to JSON' feature to make backups.
